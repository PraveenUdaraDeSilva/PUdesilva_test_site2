Ingredients

  • 150g White lentils (Urad Dal/Ulundu)
  • 150g Rice Flour
  • Salt to Taste
  • 1 cup Coconut Milk
  • Coconut Oil for frying (Vegetable Oil can be used)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Soak white lentils in water overnight or for about 5-6 hours. Drain the water and grind to a thick mixture Add the salt and the coconut milk and mix.
